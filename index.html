Our Process

From Synthesis to Your Lab —
Every Step Verified

We don't rely on supplier assurances. Every product goes through a documented multi-step verification process before it ever ships.

01
Controlled Sourcing

We source exclusively from vetted synthesis partners with documented quality management systems and verified track records of consistent output.

HPLC Purity Analysis

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, quality, and our process.

How long does it take to receive my order? ▾
We ship within 24 hours of receiving your order unless it is a holiday or Sunday. You should receive your package within 1–5 business days based on your location within the United States.
Where can I find the Certificate of Analysis for a product? ▾
Every product page on thortides.com includes a link to the Certificate of Analysis for that item. No account creation or purchase is required to access it. You can review the full analytical documentation before placing any order.
What is your return and exchange policy? ▾
Please visit our Return & Refund Policy page for full details on our return and exchange process.
Can I change my shipping address after placing an order? ▾
Unfortunately, due to fraud prevention policies we are unable to accept any address changes after an order is placed. Your billing and shipping address must match when placing your order.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept PayPal, MasterCard, and Visa. All transactions are protected with 128-bit SSL encryption. Your payment information is fully secured throughout the checkout process.
Do you ship internationally? ▾
At this time we are only shipping within the United States. We hope to expand to additional countries in the future.
Are your products for human consumption? ▾
All products sold by Thortides are intended for use in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Purchasers are responsible for ensuring compliance with all local laws in their jurisdiction.
